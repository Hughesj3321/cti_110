My Favorite Anime

Afro Samurai

Voiced by Samuel Jackson

Music Done by The RZA

Afro Samrai is a anime series about a man set out to avenge the death of his father by the #2 samurai in the world. To do this, he embarks on a journey to obtain the #2 headband and kill the man who took his fathers life. While on his path to revenge Afro encounters various combatants ranging from a crossbow wielding assassin to a robot Afro Samurai and a man with a teddy bear head trying to claim the rank of number two by defeating him.
